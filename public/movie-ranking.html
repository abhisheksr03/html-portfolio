The Best Movies According to Abhishek

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This is my favourite movie. Friends Kabir, Imran and Arjun take a vacation in Spain before Kabir's marriage.
The trip turns into an opportunity to mend fences, heal wounds, fall in love with life and combat their worst fears.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

Naina, a studious girl, yearns for some fun. A chance meeting with an old classmate, Aditi, leads to a trip that changes her life forever.
She meets and falls for Bunny, but he has no time for love.
